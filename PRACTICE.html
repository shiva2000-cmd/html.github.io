PAWAN KALYAN

KONIDELA KALYAN BABU who is popular as POWER STAR PAWAN KALYAN is an actor,writer,producer,fight master,singer,choreographer and politician in Andhra Pradesh.
He was born on 2nd september,1971 in Bapatla. He made his debut with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi in 1996.And till now he worked in total 28 movies.He has massive craze
and fanbase in telugu people across the globe. In 2013 he made his political entry and established Janasena Party to serve the people.He is a real time champion with blackbelt in karate.
 From 2013,the day,Oct 11th is celebrating as world pawanism day
